// Scene 1:** A fishing boat crosses calm waters at night; suddenly, all ocean noise stops.

**Scene Title:** The Stillness
**Camera Style:** Extreme Wide Shot, moving in a slow, hypnotic dolly. Anamorphic lens captures the vast, lonely expanse of the sea and sky, making the boat seem insignificant. The steady movement creates a serene feeling, designed to be abruptly shattered.
**Environment:** A glassy, ink-black sea under a moonless, star-dusted sky. The fishing boat, the 'Albatros,' is a small, rusted vessel, its navigation lights casting a lonely green and red glow on the calm water. The air is cold, heavy with the smell of salt and diesel.
**Character & Action:** An old Captain stands at the helm, his weathered hand resting on the wheel. He takes a slow sip from a chipped mug, his eyes scanning the horizon out of pure habit, a portrait of quiet competence.
**Mood/Expression:** Serene Isolation. A deep, meditative quiet before the storm.
**Audio:** A rich diegetic soundscape: the low thrum of the boat's engine, the gentle lapping of waves against the hull, the distant cry of a lone seabird. Suddenly, an absolute, unnatural cut to silence. The engine, the water—everything vanishes. Only a faint, high-frequency tinnitus ring remains.
