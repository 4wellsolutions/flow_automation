Scene 1 — The Deep Pulse

Raw, jagged walls of dark rock glitter with veins of ore under the harsh glare of industrial lights. Puddles of murky water reflect the beams. The constant drip of water echoes.
Action: A grizzled miner, MASON, hammers a geological sensor into the rock wall. The needle on the sensor's gauge begins to twitch erratically. A low, sub-audible hum begins, vibrating through the rock. Mason frowns, tapping the gauge. The hum intensifies, shaking loose pebbles from the ceiling.
