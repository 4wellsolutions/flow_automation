**Scene 1 — The Discovery**
**Camera Style:** A low-angle, wide shot using a 24mm lens, pushing slowly forward through thick, gnarled roots. The camera movement is deliberate and cautious, mimicking the archeologists' approach.
**Character:** Professor Ruben Alvarado: Mid-50s, wiry and lean from a life of fieldwork, not muscular but with a sinewy resilience. He's of average height but often appears smaller due to a slight, academic stoop. A deeply tanned, sun-weathered face lined with the fine wrinkles of someone who squints into the sun and pores over ancient texts. A sharp, aquiline nose and thoughtful, dark brown eyes that hold a mix of intellectual curiosity and deep-seated weariness. His hair is a salt-and-pepper mix, once dark but now graying at the temples, often unkempt and damp with jungle humidity. A neatly trimmed but practical beard and mustache frame a determined mouth. Dressed for the field in 1991, he wears a sweat-stained, khaki button-down shirt with rolled-up sleeves, worn cargo pants, and scuffed, mud-caked hiking boots. He wears durable, steel-rimmed glasses and has a worn leather satchel slung over his shoulder. He holds himself with a quiet, observant intensity, often hunched forward as if perpetually leaning in to examine something more closely.
**Environment:** The deep Amazon, 1991. The air is thick with choking humidity. Sunlight struggles to pierce the dense canopy, creating a dappled, almost twilight effect on the forest floor. The ground is a messy carpet of decaying leaves, moss, and tangled vines. A massive, moss-covered stone wall, the facade of a lost temple, looms ahead.
**Action:** Professor Ruben Alvarado brushes away a curtain of hanging vines with a trembling hand, his dark brown eyes widening in disbelief. His wiry frame is tense with excitement. He takes a step forward, his mud-caked boot sinking slightly into the soft earth, and places a hand reverently on the carved stone. His team of three other archeologists emerges behind him, gasping in awe.
**Mood/Expression:** Awe, reverence, the thrill of discovery.
**Audio:** The constant, deafening cacophony of jungle insects and birds. The soft squelch of boots in mud. Alvarado’s sharp intake of breath.

**Scene 3 — Eyes in the Dark**
**Camera Style:** A first-person POV shot from inside a tent, looking out through the zipped-open flap. The focus racks between the rain-streaked foliage just outside and the terrifyingly clear, glowing eyes deeper in the darkness.
**Environment:** The archeological campsite at night. A torrential downpour soaks everything. The camp is a small cluster of canvas tents lit by a single, sputtering lantern that casts long, dancing shadows. The jungle beyond is an impenetrable wall of black.

**Scene 4 — The Vanishing**
**Camera Style:** A chaotic, handheld shot from outside Marco's tent. The camera is low to the ground, jostled and unsteady, as if dropped in a panic. It captures the action in disjointed, terrifying fragments.
**Character:** The Shadow Jaguar (Titan Form): A colossal, 50-foot-tall feline predator, possessing a terrifying sleekness that belies its immense bulk. Its frame is pure, condensed muscle, built for silent, explosive power rather than brute force. Its proportions are that of a jaguar scaled to godlike size, with massive, padded paws that allow it to move without a sound and a long, serpentine tail that sways with lethal grace. Its entire body is covered in a coat of shimmering, jet-black fur that seems to drink in light, giving it the texture of polished obsidian or cooled volcanic rock. This fur has an unnatural, light-absorbing quality, making its edges appear blurry and indistinct in low light. Its face is a mask of primal fury, with a powerful jaw capable of crushing steel. The most terrifying features are its eyes: two enormous, luminous yellow orbs that burn with cold, ancient intelligence and have no discernible pupils, glowing like embers in the perpetual twilight it creates. It moves with an unnatural silence and fluidity, a predator that *is* the night itself.
**Environment:** The muddy ground outside the tent. Rainwater pools around discarded equipment. The single camp lantern flickers violently.
**Action:** The tent flap is ripped away. A colossal, obsidian-furred paw, impossibly large and ending in glistening black claws, hooks into the tent's frame. Marco is dragged out with a single, choked scream. He scrambles in the mud for a moment before a massive, shadowy form envelops him. There is no struggle, no sound of tearing flesh. He simply vanishes into the darkness as the creature melts back into the jungle without a single footstep.
**Mood/Expression:** Shock, violent helplessness, and unnatural horror.
**Audio:** Marco's single, muffled scream, instantly cut off. The ripping of canvas. The incessant drumming of rain. Then, absolute silence.
