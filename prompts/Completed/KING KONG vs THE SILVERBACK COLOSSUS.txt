**Scene 1 — The Desperate Act**
**Camera Style:** A tight, handheld close-up on a syringe filled with shimmering, regenerative serum. The camera follows it shakily as it moves toward the massive, furred arm of a tranquilized silverback gorilla. The shallow depth of field keeps the gorilla's form blurry, focusing only on the needle and the desperate hand holding it.
**Character:** Dr. Lena Ortiz: Slender and athletic, in her late 30s. She has a wiry strength that comes from years of fieldwork, not gym workouts. She has sharp, intelligent features with high cheekbones and a determined jaw. Her skin is sun-kissed from her time outdoors. Her dark brown hair is practical, usually tied back in a messy ponytail, with loose strands framing her face. Her most defining feature is her eyes: they are a deep, expressive hazel, filled with a fierce empathy and a haunted guilt over her role in the Colossus's creation. A small, faded scar cuts through her left eyebrow, a relic from a past encounter in the wild. She wears practical, rugged clothing: a dark green, pocket-filled utility vest over a simple grey long-sleeved shirt, cargo pants, and sturdy, mud-caked hiking boots. A worn leather strap for carrying equipment is slung across her chest. A simple silver locket, a memento, is tucked under her shirt. Her posture is alert and focused, always leaning slightly forward as if ready to spring into action or observation. She moves with a quiet confidence in natural environments but appears tense and out of place in sterile, military settings. Her demeanor is a mixture of scientific curiosity, deep compassion, and a simmering, righteous anger at the injustice done to the gorilla.
**Environment:** A sterile, cold military lab. White tiled walls are stained with grime. The air hums with the sound of machinery and a flatlining heart monitor. The lighting is a harsh, flickering fluorescent overhead, casting long, frantic shadows.
**Action:** Lena's hands tremble as she pushes the plunger. The serum floods into the gorilla's bloodstream. She watches the heart monitor, her face a mask of hope and terror. The flatline wavers, then spikes into a chaotic, impossibly fast rhythm.
**Mood/Expression:** Desperation, high-stakes gamble, a violation of ethics for the sake of compassion.
**Audio:** The frantic beeping of the EKG, Lena's sharp, panicked breaths, the quiet hiss of the syringe.


**Scene 2 — Unnatural Growth**
**Camera Style:** A time-lapse effect, achieved through a series of jarring jump-cuts. The camera is positioned low in a reinforced containment cell, looking up. With each cut, the gorilla grows visibly larger, his muscles swelling and bones audibly popping and elongating.
**Character:** The Silverback Colossus: Colossal, standing at 40 feet tall. His build is a hyper-exaggerated version of a silverback gorilla, with impossibly dense, corded muscles rippling beneath his skin. His frame is broader and more powerful than any natural primate, a monument of bio-engineered might. His fur is a deep, jet-black, coarse and matted in places with dirt and grime from his rampage. A striking mantle of silver fur runs down his back and over his massive shoulders, gleaming like polished steel under light. His face is a mask of torment; his brow is heavy and scarred, his jaw powerful, and his eyes are a deep, intelligent brown, but are constantly wide with a mixture of confusion, pain, and terror. He wears remnants of his captivity: a thick, shattered metal collar is still embedded around the base of his neck, its broken edges digging into his flesh. A few tattered straps of a restraint harness cling to his torso. He holds himself with a constant, agonizing tension. His movements are jerky and violent, driven by sensory overload. His posture is often a defensive crouch, ready to lash out, but punctuated by moments of heartbreaking confusion where he looks lost and overwhelmed.
**Environment:** A brutalist concrete and steel containment cell. The walls are scarred with deep gauges. Thick steel bars bend and groan under the strain. The only light comes from a single, caged bulb overhead.
**Action:** The gorilla, now a nascent Colossus, slams a fist against the steel door, leaving a massive dent. He roars, a sound deeper and more powerful than any natural animal. His muscles tear through the restraint harness with sickening snaps.
**Mood/Expression:** Body horror, agonizing transformation, power growing beyond control.
**Audio:** Deep, wet sounds of muscle and bone stretching. The screech of metal, the earth-shaking roars.
